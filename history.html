TerribleHack History

TerribleHack has a long a long and storied history. Here's a taste of it!

March 2015: The First TerribleHack

View the March 2015 site

The first one! It was so haphazard that we booked a lecture hall that didn't have outlets in it, so we asked people to bring their own power bars and extension cords to get power from the front of the room. You can read about some of the hacks people made in this blog post.

October 2015: Terriblehack II

We tried to 3D print a trophy for this one. It turned out small and kind of not great. Fitting, I suppose?

March 2016: Terriblehack III

View the March 2016 Site

I was on a co-op work term for this one so I can only relay what I've heard from others, but apparently this one managed to cater shawarma for the event. This solidified the foiunding organizers as the clear B team.
